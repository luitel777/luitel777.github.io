Anime report of spw 2022

Aagaman Luitel | words: 862 | 4 minutes | 2023.01.27

2022 was hell of an year for my anime catalog

Urusei Yatsura

This has to be one of my best slice of life shows. This anime is relaxing to watch and the characters are is so cuteee especially Lum. I just hope the story will progress. New character are introduced every ep but their development is kinda slow to the point where its nonexistent but I guess thats one downside of slice of life anime. That being said their song is absolute banger. Do not skip it!

Yojouhan Time Machine

If you are fan of Yojouhan Shinwa Takei or Yoru wa Mijikashi Arukeyo Otome you will love this show as much as I did. This show is not best or mind blowing as in its original series but this show will certainly keep you entertained. Its relatively short featuring only 6 eps but it has a good concept like its originals.

Chainsaw Man

I enjoyed this anime and it was a banger like I expected. They used rotoscoping which contributed to some great fight scene. Overall great action anime with relatively new concept.

Bocchi The Rock

The anime of 2022. I watched this from its 1st release thinking I would just drop this like any other hitoribocchi shows but holy heckk this exceeded my expectation. For some reason I was carving for next episode every week. The animation is so wacky I love it, I love the expressions of bocchi and I love her imagination. Its also fun to watch the struggles and progress of an overexaggerated hitoribocchi. I don’t feel bad, kek.

Lycoris Recoil

Another great anime I was not expecting. I absolutely loved the voice acting of Chika Anzai and to me great va are the only reason to watch anime sometime… but this anime is fun to watch. Its simply fun. Story is not that good to the point where I straight don’t like it. That being said I really loved how Takina was being close to Chisato slowly by slowly. It has a well made character development.

Summer Time Render

Great mystery anime that forced me to read manga after 3ep. Watch this without getting spoiled. Its a fun anime about people having fun on summer weekends.

Kage no Jitsuryokusha ni Naritakute!

If you are not watching this then what are you even doing in your life. This should be the best isekai ever made. Current latest manga readers would know that its not exactly isekai looking at the conditions but ahem. This anime is cringy and makes no sense but its literally how you are supposed to watch it. The main character is the most perfect and well designed character for this show. The show is exaggerated but the manga is even more exaggerated. Just pick your poison and start watching/reading this show. All I can say is its hella fun.

Vanitas no catre 2

Sequel of Vanitas no carte this show is about vampires on mid-european era. Everything on this show seems like tailored to me. The main character Vanitas is doctor saving the vampire and his girlfriend Jeanne who accidentally loves him. The romance is cute, “Jeanne is CUTE”, its intriguing and charming in its ways and the story and character pacing is also great. And no there is no BL even though Noe sometime acts like one. All the characters are designed great so you will have special attachment to some characters. My favourite characters became Domninique and Jeanne as they act so cute and have their own set of problems.

Sono Bisque Doll

If you are into romance sure you might like it. But wimpy main character are just not that great to me. Kitagawa is polar opposite of Gojou which singlehandedly backs up the anime.

Spy Family

If you like a mind reading kid, assassin and special agent living together on mid-european era then yea this show might be for you. (European set is better in Vanitas. Cocky fyi by me). Nonetheless its fun to watch but it falls flat as you watch. It becomes repititive and you kinda know what happens next.

Hataraku Maou Sama

Like spy if you like demon and angels living in human world catering a kid doing normal things then yea you might like it but it had same problem like spy had.

Released before 2022 but I watched in 2022

Space Dandy

If you told me that I copied space warrior from Dandy I would not care. Not because really I don’t care, its just because I absolutely love spacedandy. Its wacky, its funny, its crazy, I just love everything about this show. Dandy and his crazy adventure to become rich as alien hunters and all the thing he does is just made PERFECTLY. 10/10 would rewatch again this year.

Working

As it became more an more obvious who was gonna become the Katanashi’s lover, my fear was growing bigger and bigger. My inner monster was feeding on that fear. I could not sleep and think straight for days after it struck my head. Anyways fun show. I hatethatgirl.

Ika Musume

A squid girl who came to destroy humanity but got forced to work on some shop. This show is cute, relatable and fun laid back show. Really fun anime.
